Hershey's chocolate cupcakes

7 easy steps to bake Hershey's chocolate cupcakes (ONLY 7 ingredients)
Print Recipe Pin Recipe
Prep Time 5 mins
Cook Time 20 mins
Course Dessert

Ingredients
  

  • 100 g All purpose flour
  • 100 g Cocoa powder
  • 10 g Baking powder
  • 20 g Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 50 ml milk/water
  • Hershey's syrup

Instructions
 

  • Sieve all purpose flour(2), cacao powder(2), baking powder(2) and continuously whisk with butter(2 tbsps), egg(1), milk(50ml) and Hershey’s syrup till runny consistency is achieved.
  • Pour the batter into cupcake moulds till half-filled.
  • Place the tray in the oven for a duration of 20 minutes.
  • While its baking, prepare the frosting. Whip cocoa powder, some Hershey's syrup and butter until smooth in a large bowl. Lastly, slowly stream in milk until frosting reaches desired consistency. Scrape sides and whip again until light, fluffy and smooth, 1-2 minutes.
  • Wait till cupcakes completely rise and test if baked with fork.
  • Finally, we top it off with the frosting.
Keyword chocolate, cupcakes, hershey's
